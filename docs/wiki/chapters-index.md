## Part 1 — The Problem

The problem this book addresses. No solutions yet. The point of Part 1 is to make the structure of the problem visible.

- [Chapter 1 — The Access Problem](/chapters/ch01-the-access-problem/) — The gap between knowing and changing. Why effort alone does not close it.
- [Chapter 2 — Why Everything You've Tried Doesn't Work](/chapters/ch02-why-everything-youve-tried-doesnt-work/) — Analysis, suppression, and frameworks all fail for the same reason.
- [Chapter 3 — How the Mind Actually Responds](/chapters/ch03-how-the-mind-actually-responds/) — [Resonance](/wiki/resonance/) as the pre-analytical response that reaches what analysis cannot. First exercise.

## Part 2 — The System

The mechanism, introduced step by step. Each chapter sets up the next.

- [Chapter 4 — The Internal Environment](/chapters/ch04-the-internal-environment/) — [The space](/wiki/the-space/). Two sessions: detection and structure.
- [Chapter 5 — What's In There and How to Be Near It](/chapters/ch05-whats-in-there-and-how-to-be-near-it/) — [Signals](/wiki/signal/) and [presences](/wiki/presence/). [Approach](/wiki/approach/) without disturbing.
- [Chapter 6 — Reading the System and Making Contact](/chapters/ch06-reading-the-system-and-making-contact/) — [Familiarity](/wiki/familiarity/), [Function](/wiki/function/), and first [Contact](/wiki/contact/).
- [Chapter 7 — Stability, Influence, and Consistency](/chapters/ch07-stability-influence-and-consistency/) — [Stable range](/wiki/stable-range/). [Influence](/wiki/influence/) without force. [Consistency](/wiki/consistency/) as the mechanism.

## Part 3 — Working With a Fragmented System

DID, OSDD, severe dissociative responses to trauma. The conservatism the rest of the book reflects comes from these conditions.

- [Chapter 8 — When the System Is Fragmented](/chapters/ch08-when-the-system-is-fragmented/) — What structural fragmentation actually means for the practice.
- [Chapter 9 — Working With Protector Systems](/chapters/ch09-working-with-protector-systems/) — [Protectors](/wiki/protector/) as load-bearing walls. Why the approach must be slower.
- [Chapter 10 — Stability as the Primary Goal](/chapters/ch10-stability-as-the-primary-goal/) — Stability is the work, not the precondition for it.

## Part 4 — Advanced Practice

Once the system is functioning, what to do next. And what not to do.

- [Chapter 11 — Meaning and Organization](/chapters/ch11-meaning-and-organization/) — Letting meaning emerge rather than assigning it.
- [Chapter 12 — Using It in Real Time](/chapters/ch12-using-it-in-real-time/) — The [real-time loop](/wiki/notice-step-back-adjust/).
- [Chapter 13 — Making It Baseline](/chapters/ch13-making-it-baseline/) — [Baseline](/wiki/baseline/) formation through repetition.
- [Chapter 14 — How This Gets Misused](/chapters/ch14-how-this-gets-misused/) — The four [distortions](/wiki/failure-modes/) and the reset.
- [Chapter 15 — Copying Versus Discovering](/chapters/ch15-copying-versus-discovering/) — Why borrowed systems do not work. The Doom, Big-T, Taylor, Thor example.
- [Chapter 16 — The Tool That Can't Feel the Work](/chapters/ch16-working-with-ai/) — What AI can and cannot do in this practice.

## Part 5 — Limits, Change, and the Long Arc

What the practice produces, what it does not, and how it changes over time.

- [Chapter 17 — When to Stop](/chapters/ch17-when-to-stop/) — Conditions under which the practice should pause. Using this alongside therapy.
- [Chapter 18 — What Actually Changes](/chapters/ch18-what-actually-changes/) — [Position](/wiki/baseline/), not signal. What changes at six months. What changes at a year.
- [Chapter 19 — Symbolic Evolution](/chapters/ch19-symbolic-evolution/) — How [resonance vectors](/wiki/resonance-vector/) and [presences](/wiki/presence/) develop over time.
- [Chapter 20 — The Long Arc](/chapters/ch20-the-long-arc/) — Years of practice. Restarting after gaps. When clinical support is required.
- [Chapter 21 — What This System Actually Is](/chapters/ch21-what-this-system-actually-is/) — [The one-sentence definition](/wiki/the-one-sentence/) and what it means.
- [Chapter 22 — Where This Leaves You](/chapters/ch22-where-this-leaves-you/) — Where the book ends. Where the practice continues.
