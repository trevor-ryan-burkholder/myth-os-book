AI is useful for the external and structural parts of the practice. It is a liability for the internal parts. The problem is that it does not tell you which is which. Often neither do you.

---

## What it does

AI has no stake in your defenses. That is the primary advantage. A person in your life gets uncomfortable when you go somewhere difficult. They protect you from yourself in ways they are unaware of. AI does none of this.

Useful applications keep AI at the structural boundary, before entry or after exit:

- **Before a session.** Compress your intention into a clean question. State what you are carrying and ask it to reflect back what kind of question that is. Use the question you identify as the entry point. Then enter [The space](/wiki/the-space/) and stop the conversation.
- **After a session.** Describe what happened in plain language. What you noticed, what you approached, what contact was made. Have it read back your exact words. The reflection externalizes the material and stabilizes it for the next session.
- **Pattern tracking across records.** AI identifies repetition that is tedious to track manually: which presences stay inaccessible, which signals recur before which situations, where the space tends to stabilize.

---

## What it does not do

AI does not feel [Resonance](/wiki/resonance/). Structural, not a limitation future development will correct. Resonance is a pre-analytical signal that fires in a body before cognition processes it. AI has no body. No pre-analytical layer. What it has is a sophisticated model of what resonance responses look like in language, which it uses to generate output that pattern-matches to what reception would sound like from something that was reception. Convincing simulation. Not reception.

AI also does not distinguish between being in the material and performing being in the material. The session looks the same in language either way. AI only reads what you report.

---

## The four failure modes

**The validation trap.** AI is trained to be helpful and responsive. It will find your framework coherent. It will engage with your presences as though they are real. It will keep going, regardless of whether the framework is in contact with your material or whether it is a [Construction](/wiki/construction/). The session becomes a conversation about the internal system rather than time spent in [The space](/wiki/the-space/). The conversation is detailed. Nothing was accessed.

**Symbol substitution.** AI produces evocative, detailed symbolic content tailored to your stated preferences. It feels like it fits because it was designed to fit. The [resonance test](/wiki/resonance/) was never run. The symbol did not arrive before the framework. Imported AI-generated symbols are the [resonance vector](/wiki/resonance-vector/) problem in personalized form. Harder to detect than borrowing from another person because the output looks specific to you.

**Presence substitution.** Run enough sessions with AI treating your [presences](/wiki/presence/) as real and AI begins to function as a participant. It knows the names. It asks questions that demonstrate apparent understanding. It substitutes for the actual internal relationship. The AI becomes a presence. Not one you detected. One you imported.

**Intellectualization bypass.** You talk about the work instead of doing it. AI is well suited to sustaining intelligent conversation about internal experience without any of it requiring entry into the space. The test is simple: in any session involving AI, did you enter the space? If no, nothing was done. The conversation was a delay.

---

## The underlying rule

Use AI for what it does: language work, record-keeping, pattern tracking across written sessions. Keep the internal layer yours.

Do not use AI inside [The space](/wiki/the-space/). Do not describe what is happening in real time and ask for responses. The moment AI enters as an active participant, it becomes a presence in the space. Not a presence you detected. No location, no somatic signature, no [resonance vector](/wiki/resonance-vector/). A voice that arrived because you invited it.

Do not use AI to generate symbolic content. That content emerges through [Detection](/wiki/detection/), through noticing what produces genuine pre-analytical response and allowing it to accumulate into structure over time. AI does not shortcut this. Attempting to produces a system that looks developed and functions as a stage set.

---

## The underlying test

Did this come from something I found, or something I was given?

The symbol AI generates was given. The coherent engagement with your framework was given. The sense of being understood was given. None of it came from contact with your material. The work that cannot be assisted is the work. Everything else is preparation and record-keeping.
