Indirect interaction with protected internal states. Never direct retrieval.

The mind holds material thinking cannot reach. Between conscious attention and that material sit protective structures whose job is to prevent direct access under conditions that would destabilize the rest of the system. They read pressure, not intention. Hostile or curious, both close them.

This is the experience that brings people to the work: knowing why something keeps happening and still not being able to interrupt it. Understanding and access operate on different levels. Insight alone does not produce change.

Access is built, not forced. It develops through repeated stable contact with material the defenses normally guard. Each clean interaction registers as evidence that approach does not require defense. The defended range narrows. The accessible range widens. Nothing is overcome. The conditions change.
