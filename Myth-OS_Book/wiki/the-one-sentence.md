# The one sentence

> A system for interacting with internal signals and presences without triggering resistance.

Everything else is elaboration.

The sentence is precise in ways that matter.

**Interacting**, not understanding, not analyzing, not resolving. The system does not produce insight as its primary output. It produces [[contact]]. Repeated stable contact with previously inaccessible material, under conditions that do not trigger the defensive structures preventing access. Insight follows from accumulated contact, sometimes. Insight is not the goal. Contact is.

**Internal signals and presences**, two distinct things most approaches collapse into one. [[signal|Signals]] are what shows up in real time: reactions, emotions, impulses, responses that happen before they are chosen. [[presence|Presences]] are what exists in the internal space. Elements that occupy locations, generate signals, and operate independently of whether they are being attended to. Treating signals without reaching the presences behind them produces management. The source stays active. The signals keep coming.

**Without triggering resistance.** The mechanism that makes the whole thing work. The defensive structures preventing direct access do not evaluate intention. They read pressure. Curiosity produces the same response as hostility when both are applied with force. The system avoids triggering those responses by keeping every interaction below the threshold at which defense activates. Not by overcoming resistance. By giving it no reason to engage.

## The five elements

The system compresses into five elements that depend on each other.

- [[resonance|Indirect access]] is why it works at all.
- [[stable-range|Stability before depth]] is what keeps it working rather than destabilizing.
- [[minimal-input|Minimal input]] maintains the conditions stability needs.
- [[consistency|Consistency]] turns those conditions from temporary states into [[baseline]] behavior.
- [[notice-step-back-adjust|Real-time application]] converts session work into something that changes how daily life runs.

Indirect access without stability produces destabilization. Stability without consistency produces access that resets between sessions. Consistency without real-time application produces internal work that stays internal. The system is the relationship between these elements, not any one of them.
