# The one sentence

> A system for interacting with internal signals and presences without triggering resistance.

Everything else is elaboration of that sentence.

The sentence is worth sitting with because it is precise in ways that matter.

**Interacting**, not understanding, not analyzing, not resolving. The system does not produce insight as its primary output. It produces [[contact]]. Repeated stable contact with material that was previously inaccessible, under conditions that do not trigger the defensive structures that exist to prevent access. Insight follows from that contact, sometimes, when enough has accumulated. But insight is not the goal. Contact is.

**Internal signals and presences**, two distinct things that most approaches to internal work collapse into one. [[signal|Signals]] are what show up in real time: reactions, emotions, impulses, responses that happen before they are chosen. [[presence|Presences]] are what exist in the internal space, elements that occupy locations, generate signals, and have their own logic and function independent of whether you are attending to them. Addressing signals without reaching the presences generating them produces temporary management at best. The source stays active. The signals keep coming.

**Without triggering resistance**, this is the mechanism that makes the whole thing work. The defensive structures that prevent direct access to protected material do not evaluate intention. They respond to pressure. Curiosity produces the same response as hostility if both are applied with force. The system avoids triggering those responses by keeping every interaction below the pressure threshold at which defense activates. Not by overcoming resistance. By not giving it sufficient cause to engage.

## The five elements

The system compresses into five elements that depend on each other.

- [[resonance|Indirect access]] is why the system works at all.
- [[stable-range|Stability before depth]] is what keeps it working rather than destabilizing.
- [[minimal-input|Minimal input]] is what maintains the conditions that allow stability.
- [[consistency|Consistency]] is what builds those conditions from temporary states into [[baseline|baseline]] behavior.
- [[notice-step-back-adjust|Real-time application]] is what converts session-based work into something that changes how daily life actually runs.

Indirect access without stability produces destabilization. Stability without consistency produces access that resets between sessions. Consistency without real-time application produces internal work that stays internal. The system is the relationship between these elements, not any single one of them.
