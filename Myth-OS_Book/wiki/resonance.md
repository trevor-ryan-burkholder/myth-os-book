# Resonance

A pre-analytical response that happens before evaluation. The system reporting on itself, not you reporting on the system.

Not preference. Not agreement. Not interest. A signal the system produces when what is being encountered aligns with something already present internally. Fast, persistent, unfakeable. If you have to analyze whether something resonates, it does not.

A piece of music shifts your state in the first few seconds, before you have decided it is meaningful. A character in a film produces immediate recognition that requires no analysis. A smell pulls up a memory and you are briefly somewhere else, feeling something you did not choose to feel. In none of these did understanding precede the response. The response came first. The explanation, if it arrived, came after.

This is how one level of the system operates for everyone. That level does not wait for evaluation. It responds directly to what it reads as relevant, often stronger than the situation seems to warrant.

Resonance is the entry mechanism because it operates in the same register as the material the practice is trying to reach. Direct analysis describes from above, at the surface. Resonance enters from the side, where the material lives.

Three kinds:

- **Recognition.** Grounded, comfortable, validating. The sense of being seen rather than the sense of reaching. Points toward material already accessible. The right place to begin.
- **Aspiration with distance.** Compelling, with a quality of gap between where you are and where the symbol sits. Points toward material present but not yet accessible. Requires patience.
- **Fascination with discomfort.** Compelling and uncomfortable simultaneously. The pull and the push together. Points toward the most defended material. Real, worth working toward. Not where you start.

The practical test is speed and persistence. Resonance arrives before analysis. It returns on its own without being summoned. Mild interest fades when attention moves elsewhere. Resonance has momentum of its own.
