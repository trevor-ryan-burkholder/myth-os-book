# Resonance

A pre-analytical response that happens before evaluation. The system reporting on itself rather than you reporting on the system.

Resonance is not preference, agreement, or interest. It is a signal the system produces in response to alignment between what is being encountered and something already present internally. Resonance is fast, persistent, and cannot be faked or forced. If you have to analyze whether something resonates, it probably does not.

A piece of music shifts your state in the first few seconds, not after you have decided it is meaningful, but before. A character in a film produces immediate recognition that requires no analysis. A smell pulls up a memory and you are briefly somewhere else entirely, feeling something you did not choose to feel. In none of these cases did understanding precede the response. The response came first. The explanation, if it arrived at all, came afterward.

This is how one level of the system operates for everyone. That level does not wait for evaluation. It responds directly to what it recognizes as relevant, and it produces a response that is immediate, specific, and often stronger than the situation seems to warrant.

Resonance is the entry mechanism the practice uses because it operates in the same register as the material it is trying to reach. Direct analysis describes from above, at the surface level. Resonance enters from the side, at the level where the material actually lives.

There are three kinds of resonance response, distinguished by their quality:

- **Recognition.** Grounded, comfortable, validating, a sense of being seen rather than a sense of reaching. Points toward material that is already relatively accessible. The right place to begin.
- **Aspiration with distance.** Compelling but carries a quality of gap, a sense of where you are relative to where the symbol sits. Points toward material that exists in the system but is not yet accessible. Requires more patience to approach.
- **Fascination with discomfort.** Compelling and uncomfortable simultaneously. The pull and the push together. Points toward the most defended material, the territory the system has most actively kept inaccessible. Real and worth working toward. Not where you start.

The practical test for resonance is speed and persistence. Resonance arrives before analysis. It returns on its own without being summoned. Mild interest fades when attention moves elsewhere. Resonance has momentum of its own.
