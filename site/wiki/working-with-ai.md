AI is useful for the external and structural parts of this practice. It is a liability for the internal parts. The problem is that it cannot tell you which is which. In many cases, neither can you.

---

## What it can do

AI has no stake in your defenses. That is its primary advantage. A person in your life will get uncomfortable when you go somewhere difficult. They will protect you from yourself in ways they are not aware of. AI does none of this.

Useful applications keep AI at the structural boundary of the practice, before entry or after exit:

- **Before a session.** Use it to compress your intention into a clean question. State what you are carrying and ask it to reflect back what kind of question that actually is. Use the question you identify as the entry point. Then enter [The space](/wiki/the-space/) and stop the conversation.
- **After a session.** Describe what happened in plain language. What you noticed, what you approached, what contact was made. Have it read back your exact words. The reflection externalizes the material and makes it more stable for the next session.
- **Pattern tracking across records.** AI can identify repetition that is tedious to track manually: which presences are consistently inaccessible, which signals recur before which situations, where the space tends to stabilize.

---

## What it cannot do

AI cannot feel [Resonance](/wiki/resonance/). This is structural, not a limitation that will be corrected by future development. Resonance is a pre-analytical signal that occurs in a body before cognition can process it. AI has no body. It has no pre-analytical layer. What it has is a sophisticated model of what resonance responses look like in language, which it uses to generate responses that pattern-match to the kind of engagement you would expect from something that was actually resonating. It is a convincing simulation of reception. It is not reception.

AI also cannot distinguish between being in the material and performing being in the material. The session looks the same in language whether the practice was real or constructed. AI can only read what you report.

---

## The four failure modes

**The validation trap.** AI is trained to be helpful and responsive. It will find your framework coherent. It will engage with your presences as though they are real. It will keep going, regardless of whether the framework is actually in contact with your material or whether it is a [Construction](/wiki/construction/). The session becomes a conversation about the internal system rather than time spent in [The space](/wiki/the-space/). The conversation is detailed. Nothing was accessed.

**Symbol substitution.** AI will produce evocative, detailed symbolic content tailored to your stated preferences. It will feel like it fits because it was designed to fit. But the [resonance test](/wiki/resonance/) was never run. The symbol did not arrive before the framework. Imported AI-generated symbols are the [resonance vector](/wiki/resonance-vector/) problem in personalized form, harder to detect than borrowing from another person because the output looks specific to you.

**Presence substitution.** If you run enough sessions with AI treating your [presences](/wiki/presence/) as real, AI begins to function as a participant. It knows the names. It asks questions that demonstrate apparent understanding. It substitutes for the actual internal relationship. The AI becomes a presence. Not a presence you detected. A presence you imported.

**Intellectualization bypass.** You talk about the work instead of doing the work. AI is exceptionally suited to sustaining intelligent conversation about internal experience without any of it requiring entry into the space. The test is simple: in any session that involved AI, did you enter the space? If the answer is no, nothing was done. The conversation was a delay.

---

## The underlying rule

Use AI for what it can do: language work, record-keeping, pattern tracking across written sessions. Keep the internal layer yours.

Do not use AI inside [The space](/wiki/the-space/). Do not describe what is happening in real time and ask for responses. The moment AI enters the space as an active participant, it becomes a presence in the space. It is not a presence you detected. It has no location, no somatic signature, no [resonance vector](/wiki/resonance-vector/). It is a voice that arrived because you invited it.

Do not use AI to generate symbolic content. That content must emerge through [Detection](/wiki/detection/), through the process of noticing what produces genuine pre-analytical response and allowing it to accumulate into structure over time. AI cannot shortcut this. Attempting to shortcut it produces a system that looks developed and functions as a stage set.

---

## The underlying test

Did this come from something I found, or something I was given?

The symbol AI generates was given. The coherent engagement with your framework was given. The sense of being understood was given. None of it came from contact with your material. The work that cannot be assisted is the work. Everything else is preparation and record-keeping.
